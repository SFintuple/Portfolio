Cubix Car Rental
Cubix Cab Booking Application is a car rental solution in which user can bargain with the driver for a fare. And also make payment through e-wallet amount or card amount.
Overview
Goal
We already have a lot of Car Rental Application solutions. The gap that I see on these solutions is that people have to pay the fare that is settled by the company and they can not bargain with the driver.
Role
UX Researcher/UX Designer/Prototyper
User Personas
Based off of 3 informal interviews conducted with my firends and client, I created two personas based off common user goals and pain points.
Aayush Awasthi, Intern, Cloudlabs
Pain Points: I used to travel daily by cab, and my pain point is that I can't save any amount from my stipend because of high traveling expenses.
Suggestion: Need a solution through which I can save some money.
Shivani Rana, Banker, HDFC Bank
Pain Points: As a banker, I travel a lot to do my chores, and sometimes my travel expenses exceed the refund limit offered by the bank.
Suggestion: Need a solution by which I can minimize my travel expenses.
