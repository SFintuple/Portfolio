Qmenu
Qmenu is a startup based in India, they need me to build a solution for restaurants and hotels to minimize contact between customers and hotel staff.
Overview
Goal
QMenu wants a solution for restaurants and hotels to minimize contact between customers and hotel staff. Also, they require one point of sale/Kitchen Dashboard solution so that they can keep record of their sale and orders.
Role
UX Designer/Prototyper
User Personas
Based off of 5 informal interviews conducted with my firends and client, I created two personas based off common user goals and pain points.
Nadeem Akram, CFO, Qmenu
Pain Points: When I am on a date, and anyone from hotel staff like waiters roams around my table, It disturbs my conversation a lot and sometimes spoils my date.
Suggestion: Need a solution that will minimize interaction between customers and hotel staff.
Swati Sharma, Sr. Software Test Engineer, JFL
Pain Points: As an introvert, I try to downsize conversations. And In Dominos people, try to make orders online. So, they do not have to stand in front of the counter.
Suggestion: Need a solution that will help introverts to place an order through their mobile.
